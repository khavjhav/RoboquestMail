🎯 Round 1: Eliminator Challenge

Challenge Description:

Capture as many flags as possible in this introductory challenge!

Winners of RoboQuest 1.0 will get a chance compete at the UNIVERSAL ROBOTICS CHALLENGE in Osaka, Japan!

Objective:

🚩 Capture flags and reach the finish line within the time limit.

Rules:

🎮 Start at the designated Starting point
🔵 Blue markers indicate Flags to capture
🏁 Reach the Ending point to complete the round
⏱️
Time Limit
50 seconds

Scoring System:

🎯 Each Stage: 2 points
🚩 Each Flag captured: 10 points
🏁 Finishing at End point: 15 points
⏱️ Bonus: 1 point per remaining second

👥 Team Composition

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Team Size: 1-3 students per team
🔄 Recommended role distribution between hardware and programming

🛠️ Equipment Provided

💻 Studuino software

🏟️ Arena Specifications

📏 Length: 150 CM
↔️ Width: 60 CM
🤖 Robot size limit: 10 CM × 15 CM
